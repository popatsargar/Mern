Travelling to Jaipur

My trip to Jaipur was full of colors, forts, and delicious Rajasthani food. Here are a few highlights:

Travel Experience

The journey from Delhi to Jaipur was smooth and scenic. The pink city’s architecture and local markets were breathtaking.

Food

I tried Dal Baati Churma, Ghewar, and spicy street snacks — every bite was a treat!

Hotels

I stayed in a heritage-style hotel near Hawa Mahal, with royal décor and warm hospitality.

Places to Visit

Amber Fort, City Palace, and Nahargarh Fort were my top spots. Each told a story of history and culture.
